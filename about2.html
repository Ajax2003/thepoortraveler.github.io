What is this website about?

Information about this Website

Have you ever thought of crossing oceans, climbing up mountains, and traveling around the globe? Well, this website is for you. The Poor Traveller Website is for those individuals who have not yet found their chosen destinations and for you to have a better knowledge regarding the places that we have for you.

The Poor Traveler allows you to have a background when it comes to places that you don't seem to understand. It helps you to build an understanding so you'll know your destined location to travel to. This website is not unlike any other website, this website creates a community for individuals like you and for them to share their travels, ideas, and experiences.
